Info

Peasant cube with a tribal twist.

Unexpected synergies and unusual cycles.

If you want to splash, it's going to cost you some life or tempo (unless you're in green).

Archetypes

Tokens

Blue/White looks to create and buff tokens, leveraging spells, creatures, and enchantments to swarm the board with tokens and give them evasion.

Birds

Take to the skies with birds and bird tokens. Fly over your opponent's pathetic blockers and ride your flock to victory!

Unblockable

Blue/Black features evasive creatures and ways to make them unblockable to facilitate ninjitsu and trigger effects that occur when your opponent takes combat damage.

Ninjas/Rogues

Sneak your ninjas and rogues into combat to trigger effects; slip by your opponent's blockers with trickery or evasion and they'll be dead before they know what hit them!

Sacrifice

Black/Red views its creatures as expendable resources, sacrificing them to activate on death abilities and effects. Don't worry, you can always make more goblins!

Goblins

Go goblin mode by rallying an army of goblins, ripe for sacrifice, chump blocking, or going straight to your opponent's face!

Landfall

You have to play lands anyway, you might as well benefit from it! Red/Green can buff its creatures, make tokens, and draw cards just from playing lands.

Elementals

Connected the the earth, Elementals rise up from the ground, growing larger and more powerful with each land played. Make your land drops and trample over your opponents!

+1/+1 Counters

What's better than casting creatures? Making them bigger! Green/White permanantly buffs its board through +1/+1 counters, and also adds bonuses to creatures that have counters.

Warriors

Warriors, come out to play! Build your rag-tag army up with +1/+1 counters and send them against your opponent!

Reanimate

Put big stuff in the graveyard, then get it out for cheap. Black/White has a number of tools at its disposal so that you can get that 9-drop out on turn four!

Zombies

The dead rise from the grave with Black/White's tribe, creating tokens and using the zombie mob to overwhlem your foe!

Spell Slinging

In true Blue/Red fashion, this archetype looks to use instants and sorceries to trigger on cast effects and affect the board. Trigger prowess, make tokens, and more!

Wizards

Who better to cast spells than wizards? Use instants and sorceries to buff your wizards and draw cards, helping you find more spells!

Stuff Dying

In a typical game of Magic, creatures go to the graveyard. Black/Green uses death to its advantage to trigger on death effects. Get those things back from the graveyard and trigger those effects all over again!

Elves

One of Magic's classic tribes, elves can quickly snowball out of control due to their ability to generate mana, which, in turn, allows you to play more elves!

Equipment

Red/White looks to suit up its creatures with equipment to make them pump damage through quickly. Cheap creatures with strong keywords can pack a punch with the right equipment!

Soldiers

Soldiers are often small, but can be powerful with equipment thanks to keywords like double strike and flying. Pump them up to blitz your opponent down!

Shapeshifters

Why have an archetype when you can take everyone else's? Why have one tribe when you can be all of them? Blue/Green clones, shape-shifts, and steals its way to victory!
